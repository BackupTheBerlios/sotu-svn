The story

The game is set into one galaxy with many planets. The entire galaxy is ruled by the Empire. Each planet has pirates around it. Empire fleet is doing the best to clean it up, but you can also help and earn money from rewards. Some planets are ruled by the Rebels. You can also destroy rebel ships and earn money from rewards. If you don't attack the rebel ships, you get free passage to their planets. Rebel planets are good since trading with firearms isn't illegal, so you can make a lot of money.  Destorying ships of empire fleet doesn't give you any money, but improves your status with Rebels (and also weakens your status with Empire, unless you destroy all the ships, and noone gets away to tell what happened).

Each planet has a tech level (TL). That determines the prices of goods on it, and also the level of crime (pirates). Planets also have rebel sentiment (RS). Planets with more than 50% RS are Rebel planets, and with less than or equal to 50% are Empire planets. You move from planet to planet through hyperspace using the fuel. If you loose all fuel you're stuck on the planet until you earn enough money to buy fuel to leave it. You can fly in the orbit and kill some SOTU to earn money. After you leave hyperspace you show up somewhere in the planets orbit, and you have to get to the planet. On your way you may meet pirates and aliens, rebels and empire fleet ships. Pirates and aliens shoot at you no matter what. Rebels and empire fleet shoot at you depending on your empiral/rebel status, which can be: clean, criminal, terrorist. You are considered criminal if you're caught trading illegal goods. Chances of capture depend on RS and TL. Chance% = (TL * 5) + bonus of 30% if RS is lower than 20% or greater than 80% (planets like that are considered Planets of Stable Government). You are considered terrorist if you attack empirial/rebel ships.

As time goes by, some planets change their RS so be careful. Also planets of Stable Government increase their TL, while planets where government is overturned have their TL reduced by 4 due to civil war.


Trading system

The game has a simple trading system. You buy something cheap on some planet that has it in abundance, and sell it for a lot of money at planet that is scarce with it. Also, some goods are illegal, but you can make a lot of money with it.

Beside these weapons you have one regular weapon with infinite supply of ammo (shoot'em up concept). It can be either machine gun, ion cannon or proton torpedo. Each of these have different fire rate (machine gun is fastest) and deliver different amount of damage (machine gun is weakest). You can pick these up while you fight in the space. You can also pick up the other powerups: faster fire rate, more damage. To collect reward for pirate and rebel ships, you have to pickup what's left of them when you destroy them, to use it as evidence (kill proof). So too summarize:

You

You find yourself one day in one of the planets in the corner of the universe, with 1500 credits in your pocket. You have to decide your destiny yourself . Will you be a mercyless bounty-hunter, independent space-trader, firearms smuggler or fierce freedomfighter, it's up to you to decide.
